The problem

Many students miss deadlines because they're juggling multiple modules, assignments, projects, and revision schedules at once — on top of CCAs. It's not a motivation problem. It's a tracking problem: too many due dates live in too many places, and nothing pulls them together and tells you what matters today.

Why AI, why now

Every student's week looks different.

A generic checklist app can't tell the difference between a 5-minute quiz and a 3-day report. AI can — it reads your actual calendar, weighs deadlines against your free time, and adapts the moment something changes, instead of making you re-plan everything by hand.
